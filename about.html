ABOUT



SIMONA KATILIUTE (SRUOKA) is a Lithuanian photographer, videographer,
and multimedia designer based in Copenhagen, Denmark. She is
currently studying Multimedia Design and Communication at Zealand.
She was always passionate about capturing moments and telling stories
through images by using different forms of art, such as poetry, music,
and photography. She is now concentrating on working with visual arts
and combining different forms of media for her projects.



Simona Katiliute is open for the projects, her CV is available
if requested.
